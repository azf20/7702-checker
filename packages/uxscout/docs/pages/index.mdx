# UX Scout

UX Scout tracks improvements and innovations in usability across the Ethereum ecosystem, starting with adoption of [EIP-7702](https://github.com/ethereum/EIPs/blob/master/EIPS/eip-7702.md) and related standards.

Supported by the Optimism Foundation as part of the [EIP-7702 UX & Developer Tooling Mission](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ecosystem-contributions/issues/274).
